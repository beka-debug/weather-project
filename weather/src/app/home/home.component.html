Weather Forecast

Welcome to our Weather Forecast website. Here, you can explore weather information for different cities around the world.

Get up-to-date weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

How to use

Simply select a city from the dropdown menu or use the search box to find weather information for a specific location.

Choose between the "Current" or "Daily" tabs to view the weather for the current moment or a 5-day forecast with 3-hour intervals.
